Our Vision


Develop a new computing platform to enable interactive activities in mixed-reality worlds on a city-scale.  

Imagine a city-wide world made from a virtual representation of buildings, roads and parks in-which people participate and interact with each other and with AI entities in various activities using just mobile device.

Activities seamlessly integrate physical and virtual reality aspects into one coherent and compelling user experience.

Mixed Reality Worlds


Mixed Reality Worlds are made of humans, artificial intelligence entities and smart objects that interact with each other and evolve over time similar to how physical reality evolves over time in space.

People experience and interact with mixed reality worlds and activities using Internet-enabled mobile platforms such as smart phones or tablets.

Mixed Reality Activities


Immersive enables the creation and operation of a new kind of mixed-reality experiences - interactive activities that involve humans, AI entities and computational-based smart objects and constructs.

The ImmersiveOS core runtime runs worlds. It evolves them over time based on the behavior of humans, AI entities and smart objects and activities that operate within them.

Augmented Reality Crypto Economy


ImmersiveOS has a built-in crypto-economy features that are designed to benefit its development, users, developers, partners, foster platform growth and create the first global mixed reality eco-system.

This economy is based around a built-in a mixed reality crypto-coin called ImmersiveToken (IMM).

The coin is used to fund OS development and as the currency users users to purchase premium items, features and services in augmented reality.

Casual Activities

Raise up your mobile phone wherever you are and experience short rich-media stories placed by other people who had something meaningful to say about their experience in your location.

Get a virtual chopper drone and let it loose over your city to explore it for you. See what happens in mixed-reality through your drone's eyes using your mobile device from anywhere.

Point at buildings in your city and see them augmented with beautiful, dynamic decorations and enhancements created by artists and creators.

Creative Activities

Augment your play room with interactive toys and constructs that play dates and family members can view and interact with.
Your new toys move about the room and can interact with each other.  Cool constructs such as cranes, slides, water falls, color-shifting beacons that reach the sky that can be seen from anywhere in the neighborhood and fancy castles with moats and guards augment the physical room experiences with playful decorations and activities.

Social Activities

Team up with a group of neighbors to build together a new virtual landmark in the middle of your local park over the weekends. The construct is viewable by anyone on the platform in its location.

Compete with others in a quest to find virtual objects and moving autonomous robots that are hidden by others in their neighborhood. See how you fair relative to others and get rewarded by the activity creator for your progress and achievements.

ImmersiveOS for Publishers and Creators

Build new kind of augmented reality activities that run on the Immersive platform.  ImmersiveOS takes care of the complex technology so you can focus on your application.

Make your activities free, sponsored by brands or premium using the OS deep crypto economy features built with blockchain technology.

ImmersiveOS FAQ

What is Immersive?

Immersive is an open platform for augmented reality interactive activities. Think of Minecraft on a city-scale with social activities where anyone can participate using just their mobile phone.

What is ImmersiveOS?

ImmersiveOS is a mixed reality operating system that is designed to provide the core capabilities and runtime for the Immersive platform. It is responsible of running city-scale near-real-time simulations that involve smart objects, AI entities, people and constructs.

What is mixed reality?

Mixed reality, also known as augmented reality, is an interactive experience that involves both physical aspects such as buildings, trees and roads, and aspects and virtual aspects such as AI entities and constructs.

What are mixed reality activities?

Mixed reality activities are interactive augmented reality applications that people can participate in on the Immersive platform. ImmersiveOS is responsible for managing and executing mixed reality activities. The OS is going to include several built-in activities as well as provide the capabilities for 3rd party publishers, creators and developers to create their own activities on the platform. In creative activities people build mixed reality constructs such as extensions to physical world constructs. In quest activities, people compete alone or in teams against other people or teams on solving challenges in augmented reality. In survival activities, people compete to survive the longest in mixed reality where AI entities and objects can cause their avatars damage. In casual activities, people can experience multimedia content such as rich-media notes posted by other people in mixed reality locations such as trees, walls or community boards.

What are mixed reality AI entities?

AI entities are autonomous entities that live and interact with people in mixed reality activities. ImmersiveOS provides the sensual input to the entities and the entities AI determine its actions and interactions with humans on the Immersive platform. AI Entities can be built by 3rd party developers and participate in various activities on the platform. As an example, developers can build killer bots with weapons that humans must evade in survival activities, drones that allow people to see what’s happening in different mixed reality locations or chatty characters that converse with people in casual mixed reality activities.

What’s in it for publishers?

ImmersiveOS is designed from its core to enable publishers, creators and developers publish interactive mixed reality activities on the immersive platform. ImmersiveOS will handle the complex low-level functionality allowing publishers to focus on just building great interactive experiences. Publishers will be able to offer free activities which may be sponsored by brands and local businesses or offer premium activities that require users to pay with ImmersiveToken to participate. Publishers may award users of their activities with ImmersiveToken for progress and or achievements in their activities. As an example, a publisher may create a quest activity that will be fully managed and executed by the platform.

How is Immersive different than other AR projects?

We strongly believe that the technology stack for augmented reality should be open, free and decentralized. Today, such large-scale augmented reality projects are typically built by a large technology company using closed and proprietary technology stacks and are mainly designed to strengthen an existing online destination or a closed hardware computing platform. For example, Apple’s ARKit project is mainly designed to strengthen the position of the iPhone platform compared to competing Android mobile devices. In addition the current focus of many AR projects is room-level experiences designed for a single user, while Immersive strives to provide city-wide experiences designed for communities of local users. We also strongly believe that the next generation of online mobile experiences should be built similarly to how the world wide web was built using open standards and protocols and by active participation of a worldwide community of developers, publishers who shared the same vision for a global open interactive platform. Illusion of virtual objects placed in physical world.

What is mixed reality crypto-economy?

ImmersiveOS is going to include a crypto-economy core that is designed to benefit both its users, core developers and 3rd party developers and to enable new capabilities not possible without such a core. We strongly believe that the mixed reality platform of the future must include a strong and global crypto-currency core. The basic idea is to provide a robust infrastructure for seamless transfer of value between users and publisher and to bootstrap a market of premium smart objects, features and activities on the platform. Having a crypto-economy core allows publishers to provide premium capabilities and objects on the platform, and for users to seamlessly purchase them. It also allows for easy trading of premium items between users on the platform and makes it unnecessary for them to use offline trading platform for such items.

What is ImmersiveToken?

The immersiveOS crypto-economy is centered around a new kind of mixed reality crypto-coin called ImmersiveToken (IMM). It enables the following important platform features.

  • Award users and 3rd party publishers for creating new kind of objects, AI entities and engaging activities
  • Allow user to purchase premium features such as smart objects and AI Entities from the core dev team and from 3rd party creators
  • Allow users to participate in premium mixed reality premium activities
  • Allow activities publishers to award participants for activity achievements
  • Provides the means for brands and local businesses to pay for ads and product placement in augmented reality
  • Enables users to freely and securely trade and auction owned objects, community boards, and AI entities with each other
  • Drive forward the core OS development and quality assurance efforts by using bug bounties and developers coin awards

What is the ImmersiveToken pre-sale?

The ImmersiveToken token sale is a crowd-funding campaign to develop ImmersiveOS. It allows people to purchase the token that will be used in ImmersiveOS. It starts on July 1st, 2017 and is going to be open for 2 months. The proceeds from the crowd-sale are going to be used to grow the core dev org and to finish the first milestone of the project. Additional information about the pre-sale event is available here.

Should I participate in the ImmersiveToken pre-sale?

You should only participate in the token sale event if for the following reasons. You would like to participate in or build mixed reality activities on the platform and see the platform completed and live for both users and creators. You would like the use the token for purchasing premium features and smart objects or for participation in premium activities. You would like to support the platform and have a part in making it a reality.

What is the core dev org?

The ImmersiveOS core dev org is a global distributed group of software designers that is working on building ImmersiveOS. It is responsible for the product and technology roadmap of the platform. Participation in the core dev org is open to any developer who would like to contribute to the project. Additional information about the kind of talent we are looking for is available here.

Is Immersive open source?

Generally yes. Some OS components and related projects (such as the Immersive Studio App) are going to be open-sourced from day-one. Other OS components are going to be open to any core dev team member first while they are in early active development, and may be opened sourced over time as the technology become more mature and ready for external contributions from non core dev team developers.
