Campaign Terms

By Immersive Core Dev Team posted June 15, 2017
  • The campaign will be open for participation for a period of 2 calendar months. The campaign start date is July 1st, 2017
  • The campaigns end date is September 1st, 2017
  • The campaign is offering a pre-sale of 40% of the final supply of ImmersiveTokens - the token used on the Immersive platform
  • ImmersiveToken is not a security. It is a token that enables transfer of value in mixed reality worlds. You should only purchase it if you are interested in using it on Immersive.
  • The campaign is implemented by the ImmersiveToken smart contract on the Ethereum smart contracts platform
  • You may participate in the campaign by purchasing ImmersiveTokens using Ether on the Ethereum smart contracts platform starting on the campaign start date and ending on the campaign end date
  • The campaign is successful if the total Ether raised by offering the ImmersiveToken is equal or greater than $2M USD on the day after the campaign end date
  • The campaign fails if the total Ether raised is less that the funding goal of $2M USD on the day after the campaign end date
  • The ImmersiveToken has fixed non-adjustable exchange rate throughout the campaign of 1 ImmersiveToken equals 1 Ether and is implementing the Ethereum Standard Token standard
  • The ImmersiveOS operations org may gain ownership of the total Ether raised in the campaign by only if the campaign is successful and only after the campaign ends
  • The ImmersiveOS operations org may not gain ownership of any Ether raised in the campaign at any time if it had failed
  • You may fully redeem the Ether you used to purchase ImmersiveTokens (minus any standard Etherum smart contracts transaction costs) if the campaign fails starting on the day after the campaign end date
  • You may transfer some or all of your ImmersiveTokens balance at any time after the campaign ends and is successfull to any other Ethereum network user account or contract account
  • You may conduct any number of transfers of your ImmersiveToken balance on the Ethereum network
  • There is no pre-allocation of ImmersiveTokens. The total ownershup in a successful campaign is 40% crowd owned and 60% to the ImmersiveOS operations org
  • The total and final supply of ImmersiveTokens is automatically determined on the date of the campaign end data by the ImmersiveToken smart contract, and is computed in the following manner - the core Immersive operations org is awared an additional ImmersiveTokens 60% of the total ImmersiveTokens creating during the campaign by crowd participation. These tokens are created on the campaign end date
  • The campaign smart contact source code that enforces these terms is fully open and available for review and audits. It has undergone code review and security audit by independent Ethereum smart contracts experts - add info here - date, auditor, report
